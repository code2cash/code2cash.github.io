Attention, Interest, Desire, Action

A classic marketing model that guides customers through the buying process:

  • Attention: Grab the audience's attention with a compelling headline or opening.
  • Interest: Pique their interest with relevant information and benefits.
  • Desire: Create a strong desire for your product or service.
  • Action: Prompt the reader to take a specific action (e.g., buy, subscribe, learn more).

Ideal for crafting persuasive ad copy, sales emails, and landing pages.
